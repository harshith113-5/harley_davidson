Key Features

01

THE HEART OF THE X440

The all-new 440cc oil cooled engine makes a classic retro design statement and exudes power as well as ample low and mid range torque.The 6-speed transmission offers gearing that is optimized for both highway cruising and for city commuting.

02

BUILT FOR COMFORT AND CONFIDENCE

The frame boasts Harley-Davidson's signature wide and flat handlebars for a commanding rider position. The comfortable saddle style rider seat is perfect for long rides, while optimised chassis geometry ensures a stable ride at high speeds. The pillion seat and legroom combine with optimized pillion grab-rails ensure best-in-class passenger comfort.
